About

I realized how difficult for the people to be HONEST to own self-weakness. During my programming study, every time I began to struggle, I know I could put blames on my tutor or manager, because they should be in charge of me. but I immediately stop thinking in this way and found a fact: that I AM NOT GOOD ENOUGH now, and another fact is I am not a nature Coding GENIUS. that's why I need to learn more. Blame on others is easier but face and accept own problems needs more courage. I choose the second.
